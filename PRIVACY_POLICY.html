Introduction

Scripture Quest ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application Scripture Quest (the "App"). Please read this privacy policy carefully. If you do not agree with the terms of this privacy policy, please do not access the App.

Information We Collect

Information You Provide

  • Game Data: Clues completed, game progress, scores, bookmarks, highlights, and preferences
  • User Preferences: Theme settings, audio preferences, font choices, and other app settings
  • Multiplayer Information: Player names and game session data (if you choose to participate in multiplayer features)

Automatically Collected Information

  • Device Information: Device type, operating system, unique device identifiers, and device settings
  • Location Data: Precise location data (GPS coordinates) when you use location-based features. We collect this data to:
    • Enable location-based clues and AR experiences
    • Trigger game events when you arrive at specific locations
    • Provide navigation to clue locations
  • Usage Data: How you interact with the App, features used, and gameplay statistics
  • Camera and Media: Images and videos you capture or select for QR code scanning, image recognition, and text recognition features
  • Audio Data: Audio recordings when using microphone features for AR experiences

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services that may collect information:

  • Google Firebase: We use Firebase services including:
    • Firestore: To store game data, user progress, and multiplayer session information
    • Firebase Storage: To store user-generated content and game assets
    • Firebase Cloud Functions: To process game logic and multiplayer features
    • Firebase Cloud Messaging: To send push notifications (with your consent)
    • Firebase Analytics: Currently disabled, but may be enabled in the future to improve app performance
  • Google Maps: To provide mapping and location services
  • Google ML Kit: For on-device image recognition, text recognition, and QR code scanning

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  1. Provide and Improve Services: Deliver the App's features, including AR experiences, location-based gameplay, and multiplayer functionality
  2. Game Functionality: Process clues, track progress, enable location-based triggers, and manage game sessions
  3. Personalization: Customize your experience based on your preferences and gameplay history
  4. Communication: Send you push notifications about game events and updates (with your consent)
  5. Analytics: Understand how the App is used to improve features and fix bugs
  6. Security: Protect against fraud, abuse, and security threats

Location Data

Location data is essential for the App's core functionality. We use location data to:

  • Enable location-based scavenger hunt gameplay
  • Trigger clues and AR experiences when you arrive at specific locations
  • Provide navigation assistance to clue locations
  • Track your progress through location-based game journeys

Background Location Tracking: When you enable location-based features, the App may track your location in the background with a persistent notification. You can disable location tracking at any time through the App's settings.

Data Storage

Local Storage

We store data locally on your device using:

  • AsyncStorage: For app preferences and settings
  • SecureStore: For sensitive data like authentication tokens
  • SQLite Database: For Bible content and game data

Cloud Storage

Some data is stored in Google Firebase services:

  • Game progress and user data in Firestore
  • User-generated content in Firebase Storage
  • Multiplayer session data

Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share your information only in the following circumstances:

  1. Service Providers: With third-party service providers (Google Firebase, Google Maps) who help us operate the App
  2. Legal Requirements: When required by law or to protect our rights
  3. Business Transfers: In connection with a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets (with notice to users)
  4. Multiplayer Features: Game session data may be visible to other players in multiplayer games

Your Rights and Choices

You have the following rights regarding your information:

  1. Access: Request access to your personal data
  2. Correction: Update or correct inaccurate information
  3. Deletion: Request deletion of your data (subject to legal and operational requirements)
  4. Location Controls: Disable location tracking through device settings or App settings
  5. Push Notifications: Opt-out of push notifications through device or App settings
  6. Data Export: Request a copy of your data

Data Retention
